Golf Handicap Calculator

Use your latest scores to calculate your current handicap!

Instructions

Input your recent scores along with the course and slope rating for each course you have played at. If you are not sure of the ratings for the courses you have played at, please use the link below to access the database provided by the R&A:
R&A Course Rating and Slope Database

To calculate an accurate handicap you must input at least 54 holes of scores (three 18 hole rounds or six 9 hole rounds)
